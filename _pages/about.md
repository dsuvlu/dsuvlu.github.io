I'm a Postdoctoral Associate in [Adam Willard's group](http://willardgroup.mit.edu/) at MIT. I obtained my PhD in August 2020 from the University of Maine under the supervision of [Jay Rasaiah](http://omh.umeche.maine.edu/). On this website you can find recent personal news and information related to my research, publications, and talks.

## Recent News
* 27 May 2021 I received a Postdoctoral Enrichment award from the Burroughs Wellcome Fund!
* 19 January 2021 Our [preprint](https://doi.org/10.1101/2020.11.11.378026) about water mediated interations in confinement induced helix formation was accepted and published in [JPC B](https://doi.org/10.1021/acs.jpcb.0c10178) as part of the Lawrence R. Pratt Festschrift. I feel honored and lucky to contribute to this virtual special issue! See the Research page for more info about this work.
* 31 August 2020 I started a postdoc position in [Adam Willard's group](http://willardgroup.mit.edu/) at MIT!
* 21 August 2020 I officially completed my PhD!
* 16 July 2020 Our [preprint](https://doi.org/10.26434/chemrxiv.11865879.v2) about ligand-mediated nanocluster formation was published in [JPC C](https://doi.org/10.1021/acs.jpcc.0c04459). See the Research page for more info about this work.
