I am a Postdoctoral Associate in [Adam Willard's group](http://willardgroup.mit.edu/) at MIT. I obtained my PhD in August 2020 from the University of Maine under the supervision of [Jay Rasaiah](http://omh.umeche.maine.edu/). On this website you can find recent personal news and information related to my research, publications, and talks.

## Recent News
* 7 June 2022 I was selected for a [postdoctoral fellowship](https://www.nsf.gov/awardsearch/showAward?AWD_ID=2209216&HistoricalAwards=false) from the NSF! The fellowship will start on 1 September 2022.
* 14 February 2022 I was recognized with the [Mentorship Spotlight Award](https://chemistry.mit.edu/chemistry-news/members-of-the-chemistry-community-receive-fall-2022-mentorship-spotlight-award/) by the chemistry department at MIT.
* 25 November 2021 I and fellow Willard group member, Yizhi Shen, contributed to a project from Jeremiah Johnson's group which was published in [Nature Chemistry](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41557-021-00826-8).
* 27 May 2021 I received the [Postdoctoral Enrichment award](https://www.bwfund.org/funding-opportunities/diversity-in-science/postdoctoral-enrichment-program/grant-recipients/) from the Burroughs Wellcome Fund!
* 19 January 2021 Our [preprint](https://doi.org/10.1101/2020.11.11.378026) about water mediated interations in confinement induced helix formation was accepted and published in [JPC B](https://doi.org/10.1021/acs.jpcb.0c10178) as part of the Lawrence R. Pratt Festschrift. 
* 31 August 2020 I started a postdoc position in [Adam Willard's group](http://willardgroup.mit.edu/) at MIT!
* 21 August 2020 I officially completed my PhD!
* 16 July 2020 Our [preprint](https://doi.org/10.26434/chemrxiv.11865879.v2) about ligand-mediated nanocluster formation was published in [JPC C](https://doi.org/10.1021/acs.jpcc.0c04459). See the Research page for more info about this work.
